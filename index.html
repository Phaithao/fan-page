Hello everyone and welcome! My name is Phai Thao and my favorite thing to do on my spare time is playing League of Legends, a MOBA(Multiplayer Online Battle Arena)game. It consists of 5 players on both teams, the objective is to destroy the opponent's main building, The Nexus, to claim victory over the opponent!There are 5 roles per team, Attack Damage Carry (Adc, Long Range), Mid Lane (Mainly Bursts), Top Lane (Mainly Tanks), and Support (Help the ADC/Team during fights between the teams). There are over 100 champions that you can choose from, there's no limit as to what you can do! Join and explore the different champions that you can play from and chat with real world people!
